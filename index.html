Terrain

A beach is a landform along a body of water. It usually consists of loose particles, which are often composed of rock, such as sand, gravel, shingle, pebbles, or cobblestones.

The particles comprising a beach are occasionally biological in origin, such as mollusc shells or corraline algae. Some beaches have man-made infrastructure, such as lifeguard posts, changing rooms, and showers. They may also have hospitality venues (such as resorts, camps, hotels, and restaurants) nearby. Wild beaches, also known as undeveloped or undiscovered beaches, are not developed in this manner. Wild beaches can be valued for their untouched beauty and preserved nature.

Activities

The miles of golden sand and chrystal clear oceans offer an activity for everyone, from pasties to watersports.

Sand, surf and scenery have always been the main attractions, but a visit to Seaside is more than just a day at the beach. Come for the great shopping, to savor regional cuisine, create family memories or celebrate life with a relaxing getaway. With dependable coastal breezes and miles of open beach, Seaside is perfect for the kite enthusiast. Local kite stores offer all the equipment and supplies you will need.From bike and surrey rentals to beach cycles and scooters, Seaside offers plenty of opportunities to have some wheel fun around Seaside. Bring your own or rent from a local merchant. Local surf shops provide lessons, gear rentals and sales for anyone interested in surfing, skim boarding or kite boarding, as well as advice on the best spots for your skill level. Rejuvenate yourself with a massage, yoga class or spa treatment. Local day spas and a wellness center offer pampering, beauty treatments and therapies. Enjoy vibrant colors as the sun sets on the Pacific or take in the drama of a winter storm with majestic Tillamook Head as a backdrop. Watch the spectacle from your room, the Prom or the beach.
